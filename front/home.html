So what is the objective of data visualization?
More than 80% of the information that a person obtains through sensory organs is collected through sight. Data visualization uses visual elements to summarize and depict a large volume of data. It enables viewers to observe a great quantity of information at once that they would not be able to notice at a glance. Furthermore, everyone regardless of data literacy may readily discover data insights based on visual patterns, eliminating the need for mathematical and statistical skills for data analysis. Hence, it broadens the scope of data utilization.
